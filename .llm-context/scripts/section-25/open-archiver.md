## Description

Open Archiver is a secure, self-hosted email archiving solution, and it's completely open source. Get an email archiver that enables full-text search across email and attachments. Create a permanent, searchable, and compliant mail archive from Google Workspace, Microsoft 35, and any IMAP server.
